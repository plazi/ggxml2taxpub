Uvarovistia bakhtiara ( Uvarov, 1934 )

Uluar, Onur, Yahyaoğlu, Özgül & Çiplak, Battal Uvarovistia bakhtiara
Pholidoptera bakhtiara Uvarov, 1934: 60

( Figures 16, 21 , 28, 34; Table 5 )

Remarks: For description see Uvarov (1934) and Ünal (2012) . It differs from the most similar species U. iraka by the basal transverse sulcus of female subgenital plate and the position of the tooth of male cercus. For further evaluations see the remarks given for previous species and the Discussion below.
