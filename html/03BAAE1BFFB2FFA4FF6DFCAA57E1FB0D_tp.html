Munidopsis polita ( Smith, 1883 )

Mantelatto, Fernando L., Miranda, Ivana, Vera-Silva, Ana L., Negri, Mariana, Buranelli, Raquel C., Terossi, Mariana, Magalhães, Tatiana, Costa, Rogério C., Zara, Fernando J. & Castilho, Antonio L. Munidopsis polita
Anoplonotus politus Smith, 1883: 50 , Pl. 2, Fig. 1 ; P 3. 3 , Figs. 1–5a .

Material examined. None.

Distribution. Western Atlantic— USA ( Massachusetts , Virginia , Florida), Gulf of Mexico, Nicaragua , Lesser Antilles ( Guadeloupe ), Colombia , Brazil ( São Paulo , Santa Catarina) ( Melo-Filho 2006 , 2008 ).

Remarks. The absence of specimens collected by us is probably due to our sampling effort in shallow waters. This species is collected between 129 and 860 m depth ( Melo-Filho 2006 ). We found one specimen deposited in the MZUSP collection obtained during the N.Oc. “Prof. W. Besnard” (MZUSP 13252, st. 6693, 26°41ʹS / 046°27ʹW , 430 m ) in 19.i.1998 , and it extends significantly the southern limits of this species towards the area of occurrence of M. polita ( Melo-Filho 2006 ) . Eventhough the author informed the occurrence in São Paulo , the coordinates furnished for this specimen ( Table 1 , p. 3) seem to belong to the waters of Santa Catarina state, in Brazil .
