Sophianus alces Distant, 1904

Yeshwanth, H. M., Chérot, F. & Henry, T. J. Sophianus alces Distant 1904

( Figs. 5E , 7D , 16 F–I)

Sophianus Distant, 1904: 486 (as new species, DV, head); Schuh 1995: 16 (catalogue), 2002–2013 (online catalogue).

Diagnosis . Relatively large species, length 3.1 mm . Head black, ocelli reddish. First and second antennal segment black, third and fourth segment yellowish brown (dull flavous according to Distant 1904 ). Pronotum, mesoscutum, and major part of scutellum black; apex of scutellum yellowish. Hemelytra yellowish brown; bases of clavii darker, apices of coria and cunei brown (piceous according to Distant 1904 ). Legs reddish brown. Membrane hyaline. Pronotum distinctly punctate; hemelytra finely punctate. Male genitalia as in Figs. 16 F–I. Detailed measurements in Table 1 .
