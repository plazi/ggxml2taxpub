FIGURE 14. A–E: Maladera perniciosa ( Brenske, 1898 ) (Vietnam: Lao Cai: Sa Pa); F–J: M. sontica ( Brenske, 1898 ) (Laos: Louangnamtha: Namtha); K–O: M. verticalis ( Fairmaire, 1888 b) (China: Shaanxi: Houzhenzi). A, F, K: aedeagus, left side lateral view; D, I, N: aedeagus, right side lateral view; B, G, L: aedeagus, dorsal view; C, H, M: left parameres, ventral view; E, J, O: habitus. Scale: 0.5 mm. Habitus not to scale.

Remarks. The species was exhaustively treated (including redescription) by Ahrens (2004a , p. 276).

Distribution. See map ( Fig. 93 ) and Table 1 .
