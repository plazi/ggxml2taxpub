Remarks . This species is known to occur widely in Europe ( Brenske 1897 ; Baraud 1992 ; Ahrens 2006b , 2007b ; Ahrens & Bezděk 2016 ). The species has been mentioned also from Korea and Russia (Far East and Siberia) (see Kim & Lee 1997 ; Kim & Kim 2003). The occurrence in the Far East ( Russia ) is here confirmed, and we have now first records from China .
